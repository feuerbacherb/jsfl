FAQs

Is the Ford Escape® SUV a hybrid?

The Ford Escape® is available in four powertrain options: Hybrid, Plug-In Hybrid, 1.5L EcoBoost®, and 2.0L EcoBoost®.

What are the dimensions of the Ford Escape?

  • Length: FWD is 180.1 inches; AWD is 181.2 inches
  • Overall width with mirrors: 85.6 inches
  • Overall width without mirrors: 74.1 inches
  • Height: EcoBoost® models are 66 inches; Hybrid/PHEV models are 66.1 inches
  • Wheelbase: 106.7 inches
  • Cargo capacity: EcoBoost® models have 37.5 cubic feet behind the second row; Hybrid/PHEV have 34/4 cubic feet behind the second row
  • Maximum cargo capacity: EcoBoost® models have 65.4 cubic feet behind the first row; Hybrid/PHEV have 60.8 cubic feet behind the first row

What is the horsepower of the Ford Escape?

The 2025 Ford Escape equipped with the 1.5L EcoBoost® engine delivers 180 horsepower. For a substantial increase, the 2.0L EcoBoost® turbo engine delivers 250 horsepower. The 2.5L iVCT I-4 Hybrid delivers 192 total system horsepower, and the 2.5L iVCT I-4 Plug-In Hybrid delivers 210 total system horsepower.

What is the towing capacity of the 2025 Ford Escape?

The 2025 Ford Escape® delivers an impressive towing capacity of up to 3,500 lbs. when equipped with the available Class II Trailer Tow Package and 2.0L EcoBoost® engine. *Other engine configurations deliver anywhere from 1,500 to 2,000 lbs. of towing power.

*When properly equipped. Max towing varies based on cargo, vehicle configuration, accessories and number of passengers.

Comparing the 2025 Ford Escape versus the Toyota RAV 4, which is better?

The 2025 Ford Escape® stands out over the Toyota RAV4 for its combination of style, performance, and advanced technology. With a sleek, modern design, a spacious and high-quality interior, and an intuitive tech system featuring a large touchscreen and Ford's SYNC®4, the Escape® offers a more refined driving experience. Its range of powertrains, including hybrid and plug-in hybrid options, deliver impressive fuel efficiency and smooth acceleration. Plus, with the added value of standard safety features like Ford Co-Pilot360®, the Escape® delivers more for your money, making it the smarter choice for those seeking a versatile, engaging, and tech-savvy compact SUV.
