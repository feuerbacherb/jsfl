FAQs

What is the towing capacity of the 2025 Ford F-150 Lightning?

2025 F-150 Lightning Flash and Lariat models have a maximum conventional towing capacity of 10,000 lbs. with the available Max Trailer Tow Package and 7,700 lbs. without that package. The F-150 Lightning XLT model has a maximum conventional towing capacity of 7,700 lbs. with the available Max Trailer Tow Package and 5,000 lbs. without. The F-150 Lightning Platinum model, which includes the Max Trailer Tow Package as standard equipment, has a maximum conventional towing capacity of 8,500 lbs.*

*Max towing varies based on cargo, vehicle configuration, accessories, and number of passengers.

Does the 2025 Ford F-150 Lightning have four-wheel drive?

Yes. All 2025 Ford F-150 Lightning trucks have four-wheel drive.

What is the cargo capacity of the 2025 Ford F-150 Lightning?

The cargo box volume is 52.8 cu. ft. The Mega Power Frunk has an additional 14.1 cu. ft. of space, with a payload of up to 400 lbs. The total payload capacity is 2,000 lbs. for F-150 Lightning XLT models and 1,800 lbs. for Flash, Lariat, and Platinum models.

Why should you choose a Lightning over the Cybertruck?

Owning a 2025 Ford F-150 Lightning offers several advantages over the Tesla Cybertruck, particularly in terms of value, practicality, and user-friendly features. The Lightning starts at a lower price point—\$65,090 for the XLT trim—compared to the Cybertruck's \$102,235 base price, making it a more accessible option for many buyers. While it has slightly lower towing and payload capacities, the Lightning excels in day-to-day usability. It also boasts better smartphone connectivity, including Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, and features a more intuitive control layout, enhancing the overall driving experience. Additionally, Ford’s extensive dealership network ensures easier access to maintenance and support services, providing peace of mind for owners. For those seeking a reliable, feature-rich electric pickup focused on comfort and convenience, the F-150 Lightning is a compelling choice.
