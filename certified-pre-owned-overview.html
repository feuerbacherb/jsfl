Three Levels of Certification to Meet Your Needs

Ford Blue Advantage™ vehicles feature three levels of certification, Gold, EV and Blue, based upon several factors, including a used vehicle's age and mileage.

Gold Certified

Only Ford models up to 6 years old with less than 80,000 miles can become Gold Certified. They have to pass a thorough 172-point inspection and come with a 12-Month/12,000-Mile (whichever comes first) Comprehensive Limited Warranty,1 7-Year/100,000-Mile (whichever comes first) Powertrain Limited Warranty,1 24/7 Roadside Assistance3 and 22,000 FordPass™ Rewards Points4 to use toward your first two maintenance visits.

Gold Certified Vehicle Specifications

  • Ford models up to 6 years old with less than 80,000 miles
  • Passed a thorough 172-point inspection
  • 12-Month/12,000-Mile (whichever comes first) Comprehensive Limited Warranty1
  • 7-Year/100,000-Mile (whichever comes first) Powertrain Limited Warranty1
  • 14-Day/1,000-Mile (whichever comes first) Money Back Guarantee2
  • 24/7 Roadside Assistance3
  • 22,000 FordPass™ Rewards Points4 to use toward your first two maintenance visits

EV Certified

Every EV Certified vehicle must be a fully electric Ford vehicle up to 6 years old with no more than 80,000 miles on the odometer. It also has to pass an extensive 127-point inspection. Plus, each comes with a manufacturer-backed 12-Month/12,000-Mile (whichever comes first) Comprehensive Limited Warranty,1 8-Year/100,000-Mile (whichever comes first) BaseCARE EV Limited Warranty coverage,1 complimentary 24/7, Ford Roadside Assistance3 and 22,000 FordPass® Rewards Points4 to use toward your first two maintenance visits.

EV Certified Vehicle Specifications

  • Ford models up to 6 years old with less than 80,000 miles
  • Passed a thorough 127-point inspection
  • 12-Month/12,000-Mile (whichever comes first) Comprehensive Limited Warranty1
  • 8-Year/100,000-Mile (whichever comes first) BaseCARE EV Limited Warranty1
  • 14-Day/1,000-Mile (whichever comes first) Money Back Guarantee2

Blue Certified

Blue Certified used vehicles can be any make and model up to 10 years old with less than 150,000 miles. Each has to pass a detailed 139-point inspection and features a 90-Day/4,000-Mile (whichever comes first) Comprehensive Limited Warranty,1 24/7 Roadside Assistance3 and 11,000 FordPass™ Rewards Points4 to use toward your first maintenance visit.

Blue Certified Vehicle Specifications

  • Any make and model up to 10 years old with less than 150,000 miles
  • Passed a detailed 139-point inspection
  • 90-Day/4,000-Mile (whichever comes first) Comprehensive Limited Warranty1
  • 14-Day/1,000-Mile (whichever comes first) Money Back Guarantee2
  • 24/7 Roadside Assistance3
  • 11,000 FordPass™ Rewards Points4 to use toward your first maintenance visit
