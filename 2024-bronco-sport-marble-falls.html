Ford Bronco® Sport FAQs


What is the towing capacity for the 2024 Ford Bronco® Sport?

The 2024 Ford Bronco® Sport SUV provides a towing capacity of 2,000 pounds for all trim levels that have the 1.5L EcoBoost® engine. The 2.0L EcoBoost® engine that comes with Badlands ™ trim can tow up to 2,200 pounds. Always check your Owner's Manual for details.

When properly equipped. Max towing varies based on cargo, vehicle configuration, accessories and number of passengers.



What is Class-Exclusive Trail 1-Pedal Drive?

Simplicity of use on the trail was paramount in the creation of the Ford Bronco SUV. So much so that the engineers even made braking and accelerating easier thanks to Trail 1-Pedal Drive, available exclusively on Bronco models with the 2.7-liter engine.

When it's engaged, you only have to use one pedal (the accelerator) to control the speed of the vehicle. As you ease off the accelerator pedal, the brakes engage. Releasing the accelerator completely will bring your Bronco SUV to a controlled stop.



Can you flat tow a Bronco Sport?

Unfortunately, due to the Bronco Sport having all-wheel drive, the Bronco Sport would need to be placed on a car hauling or flat-bed trailer for long distances.



Do the doors or roof come off the Bronco® Sport?

While its bigger brother, the Ford Bronco, has removable doors and, depending on the trim, a removable roof, the Bronco® Sport has both fixed roof and doors.



Which trims of the Bronco® Sport come with a moonroof?

While the moonroof option is available for every trim level of the Bronco Sport, the moonroof option is an extra interior option.



Does the Bronco® Sport have heated seats?

The 2024 Bronco Sport has only two trims that have heated seats included: Outer Banks and Badlands. Heated seats are available to all other trims as long as they have the Convenience Package included on their order.
