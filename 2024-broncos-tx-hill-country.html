    Ford Bronco® FAQs


    What is the towing capacity for the 2024 Ford Bronco®?

    The 2024 Ford Bronco® SUV provides a towing capacity of 3,500 to 4,500 lbs. based on trim level and performance configurations. Always check your Owner's Manual for details.

    When properly equipped. Max towing varies based on cargo, vehicle configuration, accessories and number of passengers.


    What are the Ford Bronco® dimensions?

    It all depends on which model and trim you choose. All models and dimensions of the 2024 Ford Bronco ® SUVs are on the models page.

    Compare Models


    What is the difference between Bronco® & Bronco® Sport?

    The most notable exterior differences are their body styles and size. The Bronco® is larger and available in two- and four-door body styles. The Bronco® is four-door only. The two-door Bronco ® measures 173.7 inches - one inch longer than the four-door Bronco® Sport.

    Bronco Family Page


    Does the Ford Bronco® have a manual or automatic transmission?

    That depends on the model. While the Outer Banks® only comes with the 10-speed automatic transmission, the Wildtrak®, Heritage Limited and Raptor® trims are only available with an automatic transmission. All other models offer either an automatic or manual transmission.
