Embark on a journey of reliability and innovation with the 2024 Ford Escape. This compact SUV is not just a vehicle; it’s a promise of freedom, efficiency, and exhilarating performance. Reliability You Can Trust The 2024 Ford Escape has been meticulously crafted to meet your highest expectations of reliability. Consumer feedback highlights the Escape’s remarkable mileage and excellent driving feel, ensuring that it’s a vehicle that grows more reliable with time1.

Features That Set the Bar The 2024 Ford Escape is a marvel of automotive technology, boasting a range of features that cater to every aspect of your driving experience:

  • Powerful EcoBoost Engines: Choose from the 1.5L EcoBoost or the 2.0L EcoBoost engine for a ride that’s as powerful as it is smooth. The 2.0L option delivers an impressive 250 horsepower and 280 lb.-ft. of torque, promising a drive that’s anything but ordinary2.
  • Hybrid and Plug-In Hybrid Options: Embrace efficiency with the 2.5L I-4 Hybrid Engine, which offers a total system horsepower of 192 and utilizes regenerative braking to enhance your range. Or opt for the Plug-in Hybrid for an even more eco-conscious journey, boasting 210 total system horsepower2.
  • All-Wheel Drive with AWD Disconnect: Experience the perfect balance of control and efficiency with the available all-wheel drive that disconnects when not needed, ensuring optimal fuel consumption2.
  • Ford Co-Pilot360 Technology: Navigate with confidence thanks to the standard suite of driver-assist technologies that enhance safety and convenience on every trip3.
  • Wireless Connectivity: Stay connected with wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility, allowing for seamless integration of your smartphone for entertainment and navigation3.
  • 360-Degree Camera System: Gain a comprehensive view of your surroundings with the available cameras on the front, sides, and rear of the vehicle3.

Capability That Inspires The 2024 Ford Escape isn’t just about getting from point A to point B; it’s about the experience in between. With selectable drive modes, the Escape adapts to your environment, whether you’re cruising on the highway or traversing rugged terrain. The spacious interior ensures comfort for all passengers, while the generous cargo space means you can take what you need for the journey ahead.

Conclusion The 2024 Ford Escape is more than just a vehicle; it’s a companion for life’s adventures. With its blend of reliability, features, and capabilities, it stands ready to take you wherever you want to go.
