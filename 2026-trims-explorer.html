Available on ST-Line, Tremor®, ST, and Platinum®.

Best Value: Save 28% vs renewing annually over 7 years

Ships with BlueCruise 1.5.

BlueCruise enables hands-free highway driving on 97% of crontrolled access highways (interstates, freeways, expressways) in the U.S. and Canada. Built on existing technologies like Adaptive Cruise Control and Lane Centering Assist, BlueCruise helps make your drive less stressful and more enjoyable.

Includes:

  • Automatic Lane Change - dynamically monitors highway traffic to help make changing lanes simpler, and hands-free - automatically switching lanes for you without th eneed to touch the turn signal
  • In-Lane Repositioning - helps provide more space by subtly shifting away from vehicles in adjacent lanes
  • Significantly enhanced hands-free driving performance including greater ability to navigate narrow lanes and curves and improved in-lane stability for a more continuous drive.

BlueCruise service will be available for a minimum of 7 years with the purchase of this option. No additional subscription purchase will be required after the 7 years to continue service if available.

Driver-assist features are supplemental and do not replace the driver's attention, judgement and the need to control the vehicle. Watch the road and be prepared to resume control. See Owner's Manual for additional details and limitations.

Note: Select option to purchase BlueCruise hardware at the time of vehicle order. Without selecting this option, or the 1-year + 90-day plan of BlueCruise at the time of vehicle order, vehicle will never be capable of BlueCruise functionality.
