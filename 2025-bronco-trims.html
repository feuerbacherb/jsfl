FAQs

What is the Ford Bronco® towing capacity?

The 2025 Ford Bronco® SUV provides a towing capacity of 3,500 to 4,500 lbs. based on trim level and performance configurations. *Always check your Owner's Manual for details.

When properly equipped. Max towing varies based on cargo, vehicle configuration, accessories and number of passengers.

What is the differenct between the 2025 Bronco® & Bronco Sport®?

The most notable exterior differences are their body styles and size. The Bronco® vehicle is larger and available in two- and four-door body styles. The Bronco Sport® is four-door only. The two-door Bronco® measures 173.7 inches - one inch longer than the four-door Bronco Sport®.

Does the 2025 Ford Bronco® have a manual or automatic transmission?

The 2025 Ford Bronco® offers both automatic and manual transmission options, catering to different driving preferences. The 7-speed manual transmission (including a crawler gear) is available with the smaller trubocharged 2.3L EcoBoost engine, making it a great choice for off-road enthusiasts who value control and engagement. Meanwhile, the 10-speed automatic transmission is available across all engine options, including the more powerful 2.7L and 3.0L EcoBoost engines, offering smooth shifting and convenience for everyday driving or challenging terrain. This flexibility allows drivers to select the setup that best suits their needs.

How does the 2025 Ford Bronco compare to the 2025 Toyota Land Cruiser?

The 2025 Ford Bronco® stands out as a compelling choice over the 2025 Toyota Land Cruiser due to its rugged off-road capability, customizable design, and competitive pricing. With advanced terrain management systems and features like removable doors and roof panels, the Bronco delivers a more adventurous and versatile driving experience. Its retro-inspired styling captures the spirit of exploration, while its variety of trims and packages cater to both casual drivers and hardcore off-road enthusiasts. For those seeking a vehicle that seamlessly blends modern technology with an iconic design, the Bronco offers an unmatched balance of capability, affordability, and personality.

How does the 2025 Ford Bronco® compare to the 2025 Jeep Wrangler?

The 2025 Ford Bronco® offers a fresh and innovative take on off-road performance, making it a solid choice over the 2025 Jeep Wrangler. With its advanced independent front suspension, the Bronco® delivers smoother handling both on and off the trail, providing a more comfortable ride for daily driving. The Bronco® also boasts superior tech features, including a larger infotainment system and over-the-air updates, ensuring cutting-edge functionality. Its easy-to-remove doors and roof panels, combined with a wider variety of trim options, give it an edge in versatility and customization. For those looking for a blend of adventure, comfort, and modern design, the Bronco® sets a new standard in its class.
