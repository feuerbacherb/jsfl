Towing Basics

Tongue Weight

Ford recommends trailer tongue weight equal to approximately 10% of loaded trailer weight.

King Pin Weight

5th-wheel and gooseneck trailers should use approximately 15% king pin weight.

Frontal Area

Larger trailer frontal area increases aerodynamic drag and may reduce towing performance.

Payload Explained

Payload includes passengers, cargo, hitch equipment, trailer tongue weight, and accessories added to the vehicle.

Exceeding payload ratings may negatively affect braking, steering, suspension durability, stability, and towing performance.

GCWR & Safety

GCWR stands for Gross Combined Weight Rating. This represents the maximum allowable combined weight of the truck, trailer, passengers, cargo, and accessories.

Staying below GCWR helps maintain safe braking, handling, cooling performance, transmission durability, and towing stability.
