Ford Security Package (1-Year Included with Activation)

Standard on all Expedition® models.

Includes:

  • Theft Alerts - Set up push notifications from the Ford App so you're alerted if someone is tampering with your vehicle. Customize the alerts that are best for you.
  • Start Inhibit - Lost or stolen key? Going away for a while? Remotely lock down your vehicle so it can't be started even if someone has an authorized key nearby. Start inhibit is available on your Ford App home screen so that you can activate at any moment with a simple on-off-slide button.
  • Stolen Vehicle Services - Ford has your back if your vehicle is stolen. When you activate the Ford Security Package, Ford gives you a dedicated 800# or one-tap calling in the app that connects you to a live Ford agent who will work directly with law enforcement to help track and attempt to recover your vehicle if it's stolen.
  • Deductible Reimbursement - Sometimes thieves still get their way. If you're enrolled in the Ford Security Package, then you're protected by Deductible Reimbursement. Ford will cover your deductible costs up to $2,500 related to damage or loss caused by vehicle theft and reimburse you once you've provided the required information. Deductible Reimbursement availability varies by state.

Ford Security Package requires the Ford App, modem activation, and an activated plan. Equipped Ford vehicles come with a one-year included trial from new vehicle warranty start date, after which purchase is required. Requires vehicle connectivity and a connected mobile device with enabled push notifications. Terms apply.
