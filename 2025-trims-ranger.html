    FAQs

    What is the 2025 Ford Ranger® maximum towing capacity?

    The 2025 Ranger® pickup has a maximum available towing capacity of 7,500 lbs.*

    Max towing on Ranger® SuperCrew models with available Trailer Tow Package. Excludes Raptor® models. When properly equipped. Max towing varies based on cargo, vehicle configuration, accessories and number of passengers.

    How big is the cargo bed on a 2024 Ford Ranger®?

    The Ford Ranger® is equipped with a 59.6 inch bed (inside length at floor). Standard on all trims.

    How is the 2025 Ford Ranger better than the Toyota Tacoma?

    The 2024 Ford Ranger® stands out as the better choice over the 2025 Toyota Tacoma due to its superior powertrain options, refined interior, and advanced technology. The Ranger offers a more powerful standard engine, with an available twin-turbo V6 that delivers class-leading horsepower and torque, making it a stronger performer for towing and off-road adventures. Inside, the Ranger boasts a more modern and spacious cabin with a larger infotainment screen, enhanced connectivity features, and a more comfortable ride compared to the Tacoma's stiffer setup. Additionally, Ford's Co-Pilot360® safety suite provides cutting-edge driver assistance features, giving the Ranger an edge in everyday driving confidence. With a better balance of power, comfort, and technology, the 2025 Ford Ranger® proves to be the smarter buy for those seeking a midsize truck that excels in both work and play.

    How does the 2025 Ford Ranger compare to the 2025 Jeep Gladiator?

    The 2025 Ford Ranger is a more well-rounded choice than the 2025 Jeep Gladiator, offering superior on-road comfort, better fuel efficiency, and a more refined driving experience. While the Gladiator leans heavily on its rugged, off-road-focused design, the Ranger strikes a perfect balance between capability and everyday usability. With its available twin-turbo V6 engine, the Ranger delivers more horsepower and torque than the Gladiator's standard V6, making it a stronger performer for towing and highway driving. Additionally, the Ranger's modern interior features a more intuitive infortainment system, premium materials, and a quieter cabin, making it a more comfortable option for daily commuting. While the Gladiator excels in hardcore off-roading, the Ranger offers a more versatile package that appeals to those who need both rugged capability and everyday practicality.

    How does the 2025 Ford Ranger compare to the 2025 Honda Ridgeline?

    The 2025 Ford Ranger is a better choice than the 2025 Honda Ridgeline for consumers seeking true truck capability, superior towing capacity, and off-road performance. Unlike the unibody Ridgeline, which is more of a crossover-based truck, the Ranger features a traditional body-on-frame design, providing greater durability and ruggedness for demanding tasks. The Ranger also offers a higher towing capacity - up to 7,500 pounds - compared to the Ridgeline's 5,000-pound limit, making it the better option for hauling trailers, boats, or campers. Additionally, the Ranger's Raptor trim offers advanced four-wheel-drive systems and terrain management features, whereas the Ridgeline is limited in off-road capability. For buyers who need a true midsize truck with superior towing, off-road prowess, and durability, the 2025 Ford Ranger is the clear winner.
