The 2025 Ford Explorer® is the Perfect Choice for Your Next Vehicle

If you're in the market for a new vehicle, the 2025 Ford Explorer® should be at the top of your list. This SUV is not just a vehicle; it's a statement of style, power and innovation.
Bold and Stylish Design
The 2025 Ford Explorer® boasts a styling that is sure to turn heads. The front fascia has been mildly reworked with a new grille, headlamps, and lower air inlets, while the rear gets new taillamps1. Each model of the 2025 Ford Explorer has a unique treatment with some having a honeycomb design while others have a "wing" style2. This SUV is not just about making a statement everytime you hit the road.
Comfort and Luxury
The interior of the 2025 Ford Explorer® has been redesigned with comfort and luxury in mind. It features new soft-touch surfaces on the center console and door panels, and new color schemes1. From the ease of cloth, to the durability of Active X, or the softness of Miko® or leather seating surfaces, there are many options to make it your own2. The available PowerFold third-row seat can fit passengers or cargo by folding down the seat with the simple push of a button to create more space2.
Advanced Technology
The 2025 Ford Explorer® is the first Ford to implement the Ford Digital Experience, a new software system designed to easily integrate a user's favorite apps and provide native streaming and internet browsing while parked1. It also comes equipped with the Ford Co-Pilot360 Assist+ suite of active driver support technology3. Features include front automatic emergency braking, rear cross-traffic alert, blind-spot monitoring, lane departure warning, lane keeping assist, and automatic high-beams3.
Powerful Performance
The 2025 Ford Explorer® offers a choice of a turbocharged 2.3-liter four-cylinder engine that generates 300 hp and a nearly matching 310 lb-ft of torque, or a turbocharged 3.0-liter V6 that generates more excitement with 400 hp and 415 lb-ft torque4. Both engines are paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission4. Whether you're navigating city streets or hitting the open road, the Explorer delivers a smooth and powerful ride.
Safety and Reliability
Safety is a top priority for Ford, and the 2025 Explorer® is no exception. It comes standard with a host of safety features, including front automatic emergency braking, rear cross-traffic alert, blind-spot monitoring, land departure warning, lane keep assist, and automatic high-beams3.
The 2025 Ford Explorer® is more than just an SUV; it's a lifestyle choice. It offers a perfect blend of style, comfort, technology, performance, and safety. So why wait? Make the 2025 Ford Explorer your next vehicle today!

FAQs

What is the towing capacity of the 2025 Ford Explorer®?

With the Class III Trailer Tow Package standard on all models, the 2025 Ford Explorer® has a towing capacity of 5,000 lbs.*

*Max towing varies based on cargo, vehicle configuration, accessories, and number of passengers.

What is the difference between the 2025 Ford Explorer ST-Line and the ST?

The 2025 Ford Explorer ST-Line and ST trims cater to different preferences and needs. The ST-Line is designed for those who appreciate sporty aesthetics and essential features without the higher price tag. It comes with a 300-horsepower 2.3L EcoBoost® I-4 engine, black exterior accents, and a 20-inch wheel design, offering a stylish yet practical choice. On the other hand, the ST trim is geared towards performance enthusiasts boasting a powerfull 400-hp 3.0L EcoBoost® V6 engine, sport-tuned suspension, and performance brakes. It also includes luxury features like heated and ventilated front seats with a massage function and a more advanced B&O® Sound System. Essentially, the ST-Line focuses on sporty looks and affordability, while the ST emphasizes high performance and additional luxury.

What is the cargo capacity of the 2025 Ford Explorer?

The 2025 Ford Explorer has the following available cargo volumes:

  • 85.3 cu. ft. behind first row (85.8 cu. ft. on Explorer Active Model)
  • 46.0 cu. ft. behind second row
  • 16.3 cu. ft. behind third row

How does the 2025 Ford Explorer® compare to the 2025 Toyota Highlander?

The 2025 Ford Explorer offers several advantages over the 2025 Toyota Highlander. The Explorer stands out with its powerful engine options, including a 400-hp 3.0L EcoBoost® V6 in the ST trim, providing a thrilling driving experience that the Highlander can't match. Additionally, the Explorer offers a more spacious interior, particularly in the third row, making it a better choice for families needing extra room. The Explorer also boasts advanced technology features, such as the available BlueCruise hands-free highway driving system, which enhances safety and convenience on long trips. With its blend of performance, space, and cutting-edge technology, the 2025 Ford Explorer® is an excellent choice for those seeking a versatile and dynamic SUV1 2 3.

How does the 2025 Ford Explorer® compare to the 2025 Honda Pilot?

Choosing the 2025 Ford Explorer® over the 2025 Honda Pilot brings several distinct advantages that make it a standout choice. The Explorer offers a range of powerful engine options, including a robust 400-hp 3.0L EcoBoost® V6, delivering a more exhilarating driving experience compared to the Pilot's V6 engine. Additionally, the Explorer excels in towing capacity, with a maximum of 5,000 pounds, making it ideal for those who need to haul trailers or boats 1 2. The Explorer also features advanced technology like the available BlueCruise hands-free highway driving system, enhancing both safety and convenience on long journeys. With its combination of superior performance, greater towing capability, and cutting-edge technology, the 2025 Ford Explorer® is a compelling choice for those seeking a versatile and dynamic SUV 1 2.
