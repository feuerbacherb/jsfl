BlueCruise enables hands-free highway driving on 97% of controlled access highways (interstates, freeways, expressways) in the U.S. and Canada. Built on existing technologies like Adaptive Cruise Control and Lane Centering Assist, BlueCruise helps make your drive less stressful and more enjoyable.

Includes:

  • Lane Change Assist - tap your signal to initiate a hands-free lane switch when the path is clear

The 2025 Ford Explorer® is the Perfect Choice for Your Next Vehicle

If you're in the market for a new vehicle, the 2025 Ford Explorer® should be at the top of your list. This SUV is not just a vehicle; it's a statement of style, power and innovation.


Bold and Stylish Design
The 2025 Ford Explorer® boasts a styling that is sure to turn heads. The front fascia has been mildly reworked with a new grille, headlamps, and lower air inlets, while the rear gets new taillamps1. Each model of the 2025 Ford Explorer has a unique treatment with some having a honeycomb design while others have a "wing" style2. This SUV is not just about making a statement everytime you hit the road.
Comfort and Luxury
The interior of the 2025 Ford Explorer® has been redesigned with comfort and luxury in mind. It features new soft-touch surfaces on the center console and door panels, and new color schemes1. From the ease of cloth, to the durability of Active X, or the softness of Miko® or leather seating surfaces, there are many options to make it your own2. The available PowerFold third-row seat can fit passengers or cargo by folding down the seat with the simple push of a button to create more space2.

Advanced Technology
The 2025 Ford Explorer® is the first Ford to implement the Ford Digital Experience, a new software system designed to easily integrate a user's favorite apps and provide native streaming and internet browing whilel parked1. It also comes equipped with the Ford Co-Pilot360 Assist+ suite of active driver support technology3. Features include front automatic emergency braking, rear cross-traffic alert, blind-spot monitoring, lane departure warning, lane keep assist, and automatic high-beams3.
Powerful Performance
The 2025 Ford Explorer® offers a choice of a turbocharged 2.3-liter four-cylinder engine that generates 300 hp and a nearly matching 310 lb-ft of torque, or a turbocharged 3.0-liter V6 that generates more excitement with 400 hp and 415 lb-ft torque4. Both engines are paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission4. Whether you're navigating city streets or hitting the open road, the Explorer delivers a smooth and powerful ride.

Safety and Reliability
Safety is a top priority for Ford, and the 2025 Explorer® is no exception. It comes standard with a host of safety features, including front automatic emergency braking, rear cross-traffic alert, blind-spot monitoring, land departure warning, lane keep assist, and automatic high-beams3.

The 2025 Ford Explorer® is more than just an SUV; it's a lifestyle choice. It offers a perfect blend of style, comfort, technology, performance, and safety. So why wait? Make the 2025 Ford Explorer your next vehicle today!
