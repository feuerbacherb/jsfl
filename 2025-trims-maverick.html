Get it done - whatever "it" is - in the Maverick XL. The standard 2.5L hybrid engine with Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) offers up to an estimated 42 mpg in the city, while the available 2.0L EcoBoost® gas engine with 8-speed automatic transmission produces impressive power when you need it. And with the SYNC® 4 technology and flexible storage inside and out, Maverick XL delivers huge truck capability for a refreshingly compact price.
